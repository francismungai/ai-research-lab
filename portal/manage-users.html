Role Guide

  • User: Has no access to the portal (Default for new signups).
  • Contributor: Can write and save blog posts as "In Review", but cannot publish or manage site data.
  • Editor: Can approve/publish blog posts and manage Publications and Books.
  • Admin: Has full control, including managing People and System Roles.
